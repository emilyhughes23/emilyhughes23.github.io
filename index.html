Projects I've Done


Streak for the Gym

Streak for the Gym was a project for an Agile Development class sponsored by Suncorp in Australia that I took in Spring 2016. In this project, I learned the principles of Agile and then was tasked with using them to create a web application that utilized a FitBit. I was a front end developer and created the user interface using HTML and Bootstrap. Being an Agile class, we were able to work closely with our customer at Suncorp with weekly video conferences. We also had weekly showcases of our product after each iteration. Upon completion of this course, I became an ICAgile Certified Professional.

Not IMDB

Not IMDB was a product created for my Intro to Software Engineering class in Fall 2015. This project was taught using Agile development. I worked in a group of 6 to create a product for our Teacher Assistant (TA) who acted as a customer. I acted as the Technical Manager of our team and was responsible for creating and managing each iterations burndown chart and ensuring the team was on track to complete the project. In addition to my job as Technical Manager, I also acted as a developer who utilized HTML, Bootstrap, and Ruby on Rails.
